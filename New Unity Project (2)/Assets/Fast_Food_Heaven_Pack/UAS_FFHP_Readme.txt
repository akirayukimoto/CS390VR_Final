Fast Food Heaven Pack 3.0

This extremely affordable package contains 94 fast food items and prefab meal combinations made with love and care!

The models are between 6 and 272 polygons. The package comes with 256x256, 512x512 and 1024x1024 textures - diffuse (‰Unity 4), albedo (Unity 5) and normal maps. There are also prefabs for all the different texture resolutions and meal combinations to make life easier for you. All the models are available as FBX files.

This package is updated every now and then and it's price will go up, but everyone who already owns the package will of course get all updates for free.
